THE HOME FOR BRAIN IMAGING DATA OF TUMOURS

This repository aims at gathering datasets from multiple sources about brain tumours.

Download data simply registering and searching through datasets.

Become a BITR member by putting your data in the repository. This will also give you premium access to the data (typically, 'fresh' data < 1 year old are only accessible to members).

 

BITR IS FREE! but better check if you have a large project, we can host for a low cost

 

PARTICULAR FEATURES OF BITR

• Secured servers hosted at the university of Edinburgh (with backup)

• Secured login - only registered used can acsess data.

• Dataset specific licence. Use your own licence, including commercial retristriction (private company users are registered as such).

• BIT-R host for ENBIT and GLIMR
